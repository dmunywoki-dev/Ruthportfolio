## About

I built this portfolio to tell my story — not just through words, but through real, working systems.  
Over the last few years, I’ve dedicated my career to investigating complex public health challenges, tracking host-pathogen interactions, and advancing mathematical modeling of the microbiome.

This site is my way of bringing all that work together.  
It’s a dynamic, MDX-based portfolio built with **Once UI** and **Next.js**, designed to be responsive, SEO-friendly, and easy to expand as I continue building new things.

## Inspiration

After contributing to large epidemiological datasets, preparing scientific manuscripts, and delving into wet-lab research techniques — I wanted my own space that reflects that same sense of structure and intention.  
Something clean, fast, and human — like the research structures I aim to build.

So, I set out to create a portfolio that feels alive, driven by real data and purpose-driven storytelling about global health.

## Core Stack

- **Next.js** — for performance, routing, and server-side rendering  
- **Once UI** — for a clean, minimalist, and professional interface  
- **MDX** — to write project stories in Markdown with embedded components  
- **Tailwind CSS** — for responsive design and visual consistency  
- **Vercel** — for effortless CI/CD and global deployment

## Reflection

This portfolio is more than just a place to list skills — it’s my digital narrative.  
Each research project here represents a challenge I’ve faced and a lesson I’ve learned: from **analyzing vast HIV self-testing datasets in Kenya**, to **investigating Endocervical T cells**, to **developing bioinformatics workflows**.

As I continue my work as a **Postdoctoral Researcher @ McGill University**, I see this platform as a living space to explore new ideas — where science meets storytelling, and data meets public health outcomes.
