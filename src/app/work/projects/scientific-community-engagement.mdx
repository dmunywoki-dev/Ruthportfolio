## Overview

Beyond formal research, I am committed to fostering a collaborative and rigorous scientific community. This involves dedicating time to mentoring, peer review, and participating in committees that support emerging researchers and advance scientific discourse.

## Key Contributions

- **Canadian Black Scientist Network (CBSN):** Volunteer with the Career Working Development Group, helping to create resources and opportunities for Black scientists in Canada (2024 – Present).
- **Manuscript Reviewer:** Serve as a peer reviewer for journals including the *Pan African Medical Journal* (2025 – Present) and *Scientific Reports* (2024 – Present), ensuring the quality and integrity of published research.
- **Abstract Reviewer:** Reviewed abstracts for the Canadian Cancer Research Conference (2025), helping to shape the scientific program.
- **VADA Program Committee:** Member of the Internship/Lab Exchange Committee for the Visual and Automated Disease Analytics (VADA) program, facilitating research opportunities for trainees (2022 – 2023).

## Philosophy

I believe that active participation in the scientific community is essential for personal growth and the advancement of knowledge. These roles allow me to support my peers, uphold scientific standards, and contribute to a more inclusive and dynamic research environment.
