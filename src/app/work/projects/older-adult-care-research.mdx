## Overview

As a Data Liaison at the Manitoba Centre for Health Policy, I coordinated complex research projects centered on improving care for older adults. This role involved bridging the gap between data analysts, stakeholders, and policy-makers.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Project Coordination:** Managed project timelines, deliverables, and stakeholder communication.
- **Data Analysis Planning:** Developed comprehensive data analysis plans and reviewed SAS code for accuracy and efficiency.
- **Stakeholder Support:** Guided internal and external stakeholders through data access and ethics approval processes.
- **Knowledge Translation:** Produced data presentations, visualizations, and manuscripts to communicate research findings to diverse audiences.

## Technologies Used

- **Tools:** SAS, R, Microsoft Office Suite
- **Skills:** Project Management, Data Governance, Scientific Communication

## Outcome

This work contributed to evidence-based policy recommendations for older adult care in Manitoba, ensuring research findings were accurately translated into actionable insights for healthcare providers and government bodies.
