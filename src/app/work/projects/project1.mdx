## Research Overview

Conducted during my Ph.D. at the University of Manitoba (2017–2024), this research deeply explored immune correlates and host-pathogen interactions. The work bridged the gap between wet-lab assays and dry-lab bioinformatics to yield comprehensive insights into infectious diseases.

## Key Highlights

- **Data Management:** Managed and analyzed large epidemiological datasets using R and SPSS.
- **Laboratory Assays:** Performed PCR, ELISA, sequencing, and tissue imaging.
- **Statistical Analysis:** Conducted descriptive and inferential statistical analyses.
- **Bioinformatics:** Developed robust bioinformatic workflows to process sequencing data.

## Publications & Impact

Findings from this research contributed to multiple peer-reviewed publications, including investigations into how non-Lactobacillus dominance affects cervicovaginal cytokine receptor expression.

*Skills Applied: PCR, Sequencing, Tissue Imaging, R, SPSS, Epidemiological Analysis*
