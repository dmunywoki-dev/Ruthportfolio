## Project Overview

At the Manitoba Centre for Health Policy (2023–2024), I coordinated comprehensive research projects focused on older adult care, acting as a critical bridge between data analysis and stakeholder implementation.

## Key Contributions

- **Project Coordination:** Guided research projects through complex approval processes and supported stakeholders.
- **Data Analysis & Code Review:** Reviewed SAS code and developed structured data analysis plans to ensure rigorous methodological standards.
- **Knowledge Translation:** Produced clear data presentations, visualizations, and contributed to manuscript writing to communicate findings effectively.
