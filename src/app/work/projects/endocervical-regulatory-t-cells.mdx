## Overview

This research investigates the relationship between endocervical regulatory T cells and genital inflammation. Regulatory T cells play a crucial role in maintaining immune homeostasis, and this study delves into their specific functions within the female genital tract.

### Key Findings

- Detailed analysis of immune correlates and host-pathogen interactions.
- Evaluation of cervicovaginal cytokine receptor expressions.
- Implications for infectious disease susceptibility and microbiome contributions.

Published in **Frontiers in Immunology**, 2021.
