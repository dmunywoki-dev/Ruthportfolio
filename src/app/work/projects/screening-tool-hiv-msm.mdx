## Overview

Currently in preparation, this project focuses on addressing the critical gap in HIV diagnosis among men who have sex with men (MSM) in Kenya. 

### Methodology

- Cleaned and analyzed extensive HIV self-testing datasets from the Mishra Lab, St. Michael's Hospital.
- Conducted advanced clustering, regression modeling, and model validation using R and SPSS.
- Designed a targeted screening tool to improve diagnostic rates and healthcare outcomes.
