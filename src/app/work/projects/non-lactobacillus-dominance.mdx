## Overview

This research (currently under review) explores how different states of the vaginal microbiome affect local immune responses. Specifically, it looks at how non-Lactobacillus dominance impacts cytokine receptor expression.

### Research Focus

- Application of mathematical modeling and bioinformatics analysis.
- Understanding microbiome contributions to disease acquisition and persistence.
- Integration of wet-lab techniques (PCR, ELISA, sequencing) with computational data analysis.
