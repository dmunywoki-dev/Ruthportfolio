## Overview

This collaborative study examined the intricate dynamics between endocervical regulatory T cells and genital inflammation. Understanding these cellular mechanisms is key to unlocking new therapeutic pathways in medical microbiology and infectious disease management.

## Key Features

- **Immunological Research:** Investigated immune correlates within the female reproductive tract.
- **Data Interpretation:** Executed comprehensive analysis of assay results and epidemiological data.
- **Scientific Contribution:** The findings were successfully published in the high-impact journal *Frontiers in Immunology*.

*Publication: Ssemaganda A., Cholette F., Mwatelah R. Endocervical regulatory T cells and genital inflammation. Frontiers in Immunology, 202*
