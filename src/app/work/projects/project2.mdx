## Project Overview

During my time at the Mishra Lab, St. Michael's Hospital in Toronto (2020–2021), I focused on analyzing datasets related to HIV self-testing among men who have sex with men in Kenya. This work was critical for developing targeted screening tools and interventions.

## Responsibilities and Methods

- **Data Cleaning:** Processed and standardized complex, large-scale health datasets.
- **Statistical Modeling:** Conducted advanced clustering, regression modeling, and model validation techniques.
- **Tool Development:** Contributed to the development of a screening tool for undiagnosed HIV, which is currently in preparation for publication.

## Technologies
- R, SPSS, Statistical Modeling, Validation, Epidemiology
